The New York City Department of Buildings has implemented several code updates for 2026 that directly impact structural engineering requirements for both new construction and renovation projects.

## Updated Seismic Requirements

New seismic design provisions align with the latest ASCE 7 standards, affecting foundation design and lateral force-resisting systems for buildings in certain risk categories.

New provisions encourage adaptive reuse of existing structures, with clearer pathways for engineers to evaluate and certify existing structural systems for new occupancies.
